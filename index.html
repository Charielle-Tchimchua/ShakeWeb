Shake is an intuitive application specially designed to simplify sales management and improve the efficiency of small merchants. Shake helps you stay in control of your business while providing exceptional service to your customers.

Our Target Audience

The target audience for our solution includes small and medium-sized enterprises (SMEs) such as retail shops, restaurants, market vendors, service professionals, and pharmacies. These businesses need a portable, easy-to-use solution for quick and efficient billing, particularly in environments where space and mobility are essential.

Key Features

Sales Management

Transaction Processing: Handles sales transactions, and generates receipts/invoices.
Invoice Customization: Offers customizable invoice formats tailored to specific business.

CRM

Information Management: Maintains customer profiles, purchase histories, and loyalty programs.
Loyalty Programs: Tracks and manages loyalty points or discounts for repeat customers.

Integration and Compatibility

Integration with Thermal Printers: Compatible with thermal printers for quick and efficient receipt printing.
Offline Mode: Basic functionality available without an internet connection, ensuring uninterrupted service.

Frequently Asked Questions

Question 1: What is this service about?

SHAKE is a sales management application that allows businesses to process transactions, integrate thermal printers for quick receipt and invoice printing, and manage customer loyalty programs. It also includes CRM features for managing customer profiles, purchase histories, and more.

Question 2: How can I use this service?

To use SHAKE, you first need to install the application on a compatible device, such as a smartphone. Once installed, you can configure your sales and billing options, and start processing transactions then integrate it with your thermal printer, while managing customer relationships through the CRM features.

Question 3: What are the benefits of using this service?

Increased Efficiency: Fast transaction processing and instant receipt printing.
Customization: Ability to customize invoice formats to meet the specific needs of your business.
Enhanced Customer Management: Track customer profiles, purchase histories, and manage loyalty programs.
Offline Functionality: Continue using basic features even without an internet connection.

Question 4: Is there a trial period available?

Yes, SHAKE offers a free trial period that allows you to test all the main features before committing. This lets you see how the application can improve sales management and customer relationships within your business.
