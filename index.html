Frequently Asked Questions

Question 1: What is this service about?

SHAKE is a sales management application that allows businesses to process transactions, integrate thermal printers for quick receipt and invoice printing, and manage customer loyalty programs.

Question 2: How can I use this service?

Once installed, you can configure your sales and billing options, and start processing transactions then integrate it with your thermal printer, while managing customer relationships through the CRM features.

Question 3: What are the benefits of using this service?

Increased Efficiency: Fast transaction processing and instant receipt printing.
Customization: Ability to customize invoice formats to meet the specific needs of your business.
Offline Functionality: Continue using basic features even without an internet connection.
